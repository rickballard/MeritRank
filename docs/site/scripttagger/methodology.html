Methodology & Disclaimers

What ScripTagger does

  • Segments content into ~20-sentence windows.
  • Lets you tag five dimensions: TruthAlignment, EvidenceHygiene, CivicConduct, Anti-Manipulation, AmplificationQuality.
  • Builds a two-panel summary: Best claims vs Questions to verify.
  • Transcript is optional and off by default.

Noisy venues & mic profiles

Choose Auto, Crowd, Stage, or Quiet. You can run a 3-second noise calibration. Recording is always user-initiated and shows system indicators.

Preliminary → Upgraded transcripts

Live captures can be noisy. When an official video/captions appear, you can upgrade; we re-align and mark provenance. Your summary updates automatically.

How scores work

Scores are 0–1 with short rationales and links. Multiple observations can be combined; only well-supported positive credits are publishable.

Limitations

  • Transcripts and identity inference can be wrong.
  • Crowd agreement is a signal, not truth.
  • Not legal/medical advice.

Privacy

Local-first by default. Sharing is opt-in. If you contribute, session JSON may be signed and hash-anchored.
